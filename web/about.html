About svenson

For over 30 years in the Philippines, Svenson continues to provide unparalleled hairloss solutions for men and women. With branches all over Asia, it is the leader and renowned institution when it comes to hair and scalp care programs, offering the latest technologically advanced, non-surgical solutions for hairloss, baldness, and everything else concerning the hair and scalp.

With its team of qualified trichologists (hair and scalp experts), Svenson specializes in treating hairloss and scalp problems with correct diagnosis and proven effective treatments. The focus of these treatments is the early detection and elimination of the conditions that cause hairloss, clean out toxins and debris on the scalp, and to provide the ideal environment on the scalp and within the hair follicle to strengthen hair roots and stimulate stronger, healthier hair growth. For advanced baldness, Svenson offers undetectable hair replacement systems that give clients a natural-looking full head of hair once more.

Regardless of other people’s claims, hair can not be grown on a bald head (if caused by Male Pattern Baldness), but at Svenson, baldness can be prevented and hairloss can be stopped. We ensure hair stays the way it should be – strong and healthy.
