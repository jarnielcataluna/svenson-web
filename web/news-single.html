The Christmas season brings joyous parties, reunions, and wonderful feasts. It’s that time of the year when we make sure to look our best during company events, special dinners, and family gatherings. But with the cold and dry air this season, your hair is more prone to damage due to frequent styling. This is a link

Without a weather-appropriate hair care regimen, you might end up with a whole season’s worth of bad hair days. So to keep your hair healthy this holiday season, we’ve come up with a detailed guide to help you prevent hair damage.

Here are 5 hair care tips that can help keep your hair healthy this holiday season:

1. Always Use a Conditioner
The cold holiday weather can strip your hair of its natural moisture, causing dry locks, frizz, and even dandruff. To keep your hair healthy and moisturized, use a conditioner daily. This replaces lost lipids and proteins inside your hair shaft that make it soft and lustrous. It also helps seal in your hair’s cuticles to keep the ends from becoming dry.

2. Lessen Heat Styling This Season
Styling your tresses with a blow dryer, curling iron, or a flat iron this season can make your hair drier. This can irritate your scalp and cause your hair to fall out. Instead, blow dry your hair using the cool setting, or gently dry your hair with a soft towel. Do not rub your hair because friction can cause hair breakage. Remember, your hair is at its weakest when it’s wet.

3. Use Heat Protectant Products for Your Locks
If you really can’t avoid using a blow dryer or a curling iron, make sure to use a good heat protectant product for your hair. A heat protectant adds moisture to your locks, forming a protective barrier from the heat. It helps reduce moisture loss when heat is applied to your hair shafts.

4. Don’t Color Dry and Brittle Hair
